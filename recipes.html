Caramelized Onions

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 50 mins for every two onions

Ingredients

  • Sliced onions (for diced remove 5 mins cook time, but add 5 mins prep time)
  • Salt
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ground Peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Heat wide pan with oil. I like avocado oil because it has a high smoke point
  2. Once pan is hot - but before it smokes - add the onions
  3. Reduce heat from 5.5/10 to 3.5/10
  4. Gradually add seasonings as you cook. This allows gradual caramelization, and you can season to taste
  5. Enjoy onions

Lomo Saltado

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak strips
  • Red onions and tomatoes, sliced thick
  • Soy sauce and aji amarillo paste
  • French fries (served mixed in or on the side)

Instructions

  1. Sear the beef quickly in a very hot wok or skillet. Remove and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry the onions and tomatoes until slightly softened but still crisp.
  3. Add the beef back in along with soy sauce and aji amarillo.
  4. Serve immediately over rice with french fries.

Achari Gobhi

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower florets
  • Pickling spices (fennel, mustard seeds, nigella seeds)
  • Ginger, garlic, and green chilies
  • Mustard oil

Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil until it smokes slightly, then let it cool a bit.
  2. Temper the pickling spices in the oil.
  3. Add ginger, garlic, and the cauliflower.
  4. Sauté until tender and coated with the aromatic spices.

Handmade Dumplings

Prep time: 60 mins | Cook time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Ground pork or chicken
  • Napa cabbage, finely chopped and squeezed dry
  • Ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil
  • Dumpling wrappers

Instructions

  1. Mix the filling ingredients thoroughly in one direction.
  2. Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of a wrapper.
  3. Wet the edges with water, fold, and pleat to seal.
  4. Pan-fry, boil, or steam until cooked through.
